About AgriAssist

AgriAssist is an innovative Multilingual GenAI Chatbot specifically designed for farmers. Our goal is to bridge the information gap by providing a smart, easy-to-use, and localized assistant that understands regional agricultural contexts.

Our Mission

To empower farmers with modern technology, helping them make informed decisions about crop management, soil health, pest control, and market trends, thereby increasing yield and sustainability.
